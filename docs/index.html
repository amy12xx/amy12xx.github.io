I am a staff data scientist at Jasper.ai, where I build and fine tune large language models on Jasper data, work closely with Product to integrate AI and evaluate and lead some of Jasper's LLM partnerships. I led the AI solution development for Jasper Brand Voice. I created an LM-evaluation-harness (on top of EAI's LM harness) to evaluate LLMs on marketing content use-cases, and created custom metrics to support it.

Before this, I was a lead data scientist at Fractal.ai, where I led a team working on natural language processing and text mining problems, for industry, and applied research. Prior to that, I was a Technology Lead at Infosys, and part of the Machine Learning Center of Excellence.

I have a Masters in CS, with a specialization in Machine Learning from Georgia Institute of Technology. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from PCCE, Goa.

Research interests

In the past few months, I have been deeply involved in researching ways to evaluate LLMs effectively on real-world use cases (such as creative content generation). My other interests lie in Reinforcement Learning, Imitation Learning and Generalization. I am also motivated in the diagnosis of models, and policies. My work experience lies in NLP and text mining, where I built and deployed solutions for industry applications in these areas.
