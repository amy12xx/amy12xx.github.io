I am a recent graduate student of Computer Science (OMSCS), with a specialization in Machine Learning from Georgia Institute of Technology. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from PCCE, Goa.

Research interests

My interests lie broadly in Reinforcement Learning, Representation Learning and Natural Language Processing. My current focus is in Imitation Learning, and Generalization. I am also motivated in the diagnosis of models, and policies. My work experience lies in NLP and text mining, where I built and deployed solutions for industry applications in these areas.
